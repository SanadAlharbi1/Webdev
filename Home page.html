Welcome to our website





















Our website focuses on tourism in the north and northwest of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We aim to promote and highlight the beauty and diversity of the northern regions of the Kingdom as a wonderful tourist destination.


North and northwest Saudi Arabia are home to a variety of stunning natural attractions and important historical sites. From towering mountains and green valleys to charming beaches, snow-covered mountains in winter, and natural reserves abundant with wildlife, the northern regions embrace unique and exciting tourist experiences
