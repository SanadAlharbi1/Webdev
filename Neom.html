Launched in 2017 by His Royal Highness Prince Mohammed bin Salman bin Abdulaziz, Crown Prince and Prime Minister, NEOM is a futuristic region in northwest Saudi Arabia powered by 100% renewable energy. NEOM is a place that prioritizes people and nature, creating a new model for sustainable living, working, and prospering. It is a place where humanity can progress without compromising the health of the planet.

NEOM is home to THE LINE, Oxagon, Trojena, and Sindalah.
